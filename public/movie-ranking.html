The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Hitman's Wife's Bodyguard

The bodyguard, Michael Bryce, continues his friendship with assassin, Darius Kincaid, as they try to save Darius' wife Sonia.

12th Fail

The real-life story of IPS Officer Manoj Kumar Sharma and IRS Officer Shraddha Joshi.

3 idiots

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently, even as the rest of the world called them "idiots".
